     About Me

    I am currently a senior computer scientist at Shenzhen Institute of Computing Sciences (SICS), based in Shenzhen, China. My research interests cover a broad area related to decentralized and/or distributed systems and their performance, including

    • Systems for graph analytics;
    • System security and privacy;
    • Distributed systems and cloud computing;
    • Software-defined networking and datacenter networking.
