Format for Project Proposal

 

 

This is a suggested way of structuring the proposal that you will submit for your course project. This is only suggested. Also, as you start doing what you propose, you will almost surely realize you have to make some changes, sometime small and sometime big.

 

1.       What is the broad motivation behind the project?

2.       What is the specific problem statement for your project?

3.       What has been done before?

4.       What is your solution approach?

5.       What specifically do you plan to do?

6.       How would you demonstrate what you do?

 

Length: Suggested length of 2 pages in IEEE double column format, not including references. This is a suggested length only.
